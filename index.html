About Free Time Ventures

Free Time Ventures is dedicated to building innovative technology products for health and productivity. We specialize in partnering with engineers to create transformative businesses, focusing on go-to-market strategies, distribution, and impactful marketing efforts.

About the Founder

The founder of Free Time Ventures is an accomplished engineer with a decade of experience at Microsoft, where they contributed to transformative products like Teams, Outlook, and Office. Passionate about innovation, they transitioned to pursuing their dream of building impactful ventures with a mission to unlock value in health and productivity.

With a history of success in software development, the founder's work includes top-rated mobile apps featured in publications like TIME and Mashable, high-performing Shopify solutions, and widely acclaimed websites. Their academic achievements include being a Gold Medalist and recipient of the prestigious GE Foundation Scholarship.

In addition to their technical expertise, the founder has a deep commitment to community and social impact, demonstrated through leadership roles in nonprofit initiatives and advocacy for underserved populations. At Free Time Ventures, they bring a visionary approach to empowering teams, developing innovative technologies, and exploring untapped opportunities in the health and productivity sectors.
