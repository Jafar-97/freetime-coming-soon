About the Founder

The founder of Free Time Ventures is an accomplished engineer with a decade of experience at Microsoft, where they contributed to transformative products like Teams, Outlook, and Office. Passionate about innovation, they transitioned to pursuing their dream of building impactful ventures with a mission to unlock value in health and productivity.

With a history of success in software development, the founder's work includes top-rated mobile apps featured in publications like TIME and Mashable, high-performing Shopify solutions, and widely acclaimed websites. Their academic achievements include being a Gold Medalist and recipient of the prestigious GE Foundation Scholarship.
