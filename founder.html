Mohammad Shahbaz Moosa

Mohammad Shahbaz Moosa is a visionary technologist and entrepreneur dedicated to helping others achieve their dreams. His career journey began with fulfilling his first dream: working as an engineer at Microsoft for over a decade. During his tenure, he contributed to world-class applications like Teams, Outlook, and Office, collaborating with some of the brightest minds in technology to amplify human impact through innovation.

In December 2024, Mohammad transitioned to working full-time on his passion project, Free Time Ventures, fulfilling his second dream of living and working in Makkah. At Free Time Ventures, he is driven by the ambition to create impactful products and inspire others to pursue their aspirations. As he often says, "Dream #3 is where the magic begins."
